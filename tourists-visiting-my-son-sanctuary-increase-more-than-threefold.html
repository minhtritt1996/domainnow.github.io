Tourists Visiting Mỹ Sơn Sanctuary Increase More Than Threefold

Tran Minh Tri December 30, 2024

In 2023, Mỹ Sơn Sanctuary welcomed 388,000 visitors, marking a remarkable increase of over 344% compared to the same period in 2022. Revenue from ticket sales also surged, reaching over 69 billion VND.

This significant growth highlights the recovery and rising appeal of Mỹ Sơn Sanctuary as a tourist destination. Recognized as a UNESCO World Cultural Heritage site, Mỹ Sơn attracts both domestic and international visitors thanks to its rich history, unique Champa architecture, and tranquil natural surroundings.

To maintain this momentum, the site has continuously enhanced its visitor experience by introducing new programs and services. Cultural performances such as "Legendary Apsara" and traditional Champa dance shows have become highlights, providing tourists with deeper insights into Champa culture.

Additionally, Mỹ Sơn has focused on preserving its architectural and spiritual heritage. Efforts include restoring damaged structures, improving infrastructure, and expanding promotional activities to introduce the sanctuary to a broader audience.

Despite these achievements, experts suggest that further improvements are needed to retain visitors for longer durations. Enhancing supplementary services and diversifying the experiences available at Mỹ Sơn are seen as key steps to ensure sustainable development for this historical site.

As visitor numbers continue to rise, Mỹ Sơn Sanctuary reaffirms its position as one of Vietnam’s most iconic cultural and historical landmarks.
